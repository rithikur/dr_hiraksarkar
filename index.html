// About

I am an Assistant Professor III in Mechatronics Engineering at KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology, Coimbatore. My Ph.D. (Techno India University, 2023) investigated early detection of solar flares and cyclonic formation using signal analysis and radar data.

My research bridges space physics and intelligent systems — from magnetohydrodynamics and VLF atmospheric signals to brain-computer interfaces, robotic automation, and machine-learning-driven climate prediction. 9+ years of research. 8+ years of teaching.
